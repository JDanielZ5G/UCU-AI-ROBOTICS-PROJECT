ROBOTICS

Is branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots, which are machines that can be use to perform specific or multiple tasks.

In UCU, there has been a number of activities the have shown a display of activities in the robotics chapter.

On 23/03/2024 there was a virtual reality experience at the exhibition at UCU.
On the 14/05/2024 there was a display of robotic wheel chair. In addition they staged a display of the rapid reserve Robot.

In UCU the robotics chapter has evolved from a variety of robots starting with the robotic wheel chair to the Reserve robot now to the one called "Tiana".Tiana was once a project but now a humanoid robot powered by high voltage bus servos aluminium alloy brackets and HD wide angle cameras. Tiana welcomes and greets guests at the department of computing Tianah can run play soccer sing and sort things. Tianah can also speak a variety of languages like English, Spanish and French.

The students in the robotics chapter are working hard to train Tiana all local languages with time.
